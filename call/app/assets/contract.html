Fall Exhibition, 2013 Running November 29 through December 22.

If you would like to participate in the Fall Exhibition, please review the following guidelines, then follow the link at the bottom of this page to sign up and pay online.

All work must be delivered to the Gallery on Nov. 23 or 24, between the hours of 10am and 4pm. Work must be picked up after the closing gala on Dec 22. If this is not feasible, the Lender is responsible for arranging an alternative time and location for delivery of the artwork. For all sales, the Lender is responsible for shipping, packaging, and/or delivery of artwork to the buyer.

The Gallery may reproduce the work in publications and promotional material associated with the exhibition, unless otherwise communicated in writing.
